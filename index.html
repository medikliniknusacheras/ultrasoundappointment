Available Ultrasound Services

Thyroid Scan - RM90

A thyroid scan is used to check the thyroid gland for abnormalities, such as lumps or inflammation.

Kidneys, Ureter, Bladder Scan (KUB) - RM100

KUB scan is used to examine the kidneys, ureters, and bladder for stones, blockages, or other issues.

Hepatobiliary Scan (HBS) - RM100

This scan looks at the liver, gallbladder, bile ducts, and bile flow.

Breast Scan - RM100

A breast scan is used to detect any abnormalities or changes in breast tissue.

Scrotum Scan - RM100

This scan examines the scrotum and testes for abnormalities such as tumors or varicoceles.

Single Lower Limb + Doppler - RM100

This scan evaluates the blood flow in the lower limb, checking for any blockages or clots.

Swelling Scan - RM60

This scan is used to investigate the cause of swelling in various parts of the body.

Whole Abdomen Scan - RM250

A comprehensive scan of the entire abdomen, including all major organs.

Lower Abdomen Scan - RM120

This scan focuses on the lower abdomen to check for issues in the uterus, ovaries, and bladder.

Upper Abdomen Scan - RM150

This scan examines the upper abdomen, including the liver, gallbladder, pancreas, and spleen.

Antenatal Ultrasound Services

Early Scan (5 weeks to 18 weeks) - RM40

Includes photo. Used to confirm pregnancy, check the baby’s heartbeat, and determine the gestational age.

2D Scan + Gender (18 weeks to 40 weeks) - RM50

Includes photo. Determines the baby's gender and checks for overall development.

3D Scan (24 weeks to 34 weeks) - RM90

Includes CD + photo. Provides a three-dimensional image of the baby, giving a more detailed view.

Detail Scan (18 weeks to 24 weeks) - RM120

Includes report + photo. A comprehensive scan that checks for physical abnormalities and development issues.

Detail Scan + 3D 4D 5D Scan (24 weeks to 34 weeks) - RM160

Includes CD + report + photo. Provides detailed images and videos of the baby, allowing for an in-depth examination.
